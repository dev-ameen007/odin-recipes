Burger

Description

A classic and delicious homemade burger recipe that's perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef - 1/2 lb
  • Burger buns - 2
  • Lettuce leaves - 2
  • Sliced tomatoes - 2
  • Sliced onions - 1/2
  • Sliced cheese - 2 slices
  • Ketchup - 2 tbsp
  • Mustard - 1 tbsp
  • Mayonnaise - 1 tbsp
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  • Black pepper - 1/4 tsp

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan.
  2. Divide the ground beef into two equal portions and form them into burger patties.
  3. Season the patties with salt and black pepper.
  4. Cook the patties on the grill or pan until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. While the patties are cooking, split the burger buns and lightly toast them on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the inside of the bun halves.
  7. Place lettuce leaves, sliced tomatoes, and sliced onions on the bottom bun.
  8. Once the patties are cooked, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt slightly.
  9. Put the cooked burger patties on top of the vegetables.
  10. Close the burger with the top bun.
  11. Enjoy your homemade burger!
